Known ActivitiesModules generally found at the N-terminus of a GH16 module (itself frequently lacking a catalytic machinery) and more seldomly in isolation. The β-1,3-glucan binding function has been demonstrated, along with binding to lipopolysaccharide and lipoteichoic acid.
